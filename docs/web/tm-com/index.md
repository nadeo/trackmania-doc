# Trackmania Website

The [main Trackmania website](https://trackmania.com) contains plenty of pages and tools that allow you to check on as well as manage in-game content.

## Campaign

The **Campaign** page displays details about the campaigns as well as the your World/Continent/Country/Region rankings and medal count.

The banner changes depending on the season. The season can be changed by using the buttons on the left and right of the banner.

Maps in the campaign are displayed below in a 5x5 grid. Each map contains information about the maps name, your achieved medals and your time on the map.

Clicking a maps thumbnail will bring you to the respective map page.

## Track of the Day

On the **Track of the Day** page, you will find all maps that were picked as a Track of the Day grouped by year and month. 

The banner contains the thumbnail of the most recently picked map as well as the amount of medals gained during the selected month. The month can be changed by using the buttons on the left and right on the banner.

TOTDs will be displayed in a grid in a calendar like manner - each map is tagged with the day it was picked. Infobox under the maps display your medals as well as the map's name and author.

Clicking a maps thumbnail will bring you to the respective map page.

## Esports

The **Esports** section contains information regarding the latest Trackmania Esports Circuit. 

This is the main resource for all pro play in Trackmania - you'll find the most important details here, like rulebooks for competitions and rankings.

The news ticker shows all posts tagged as Esports - it can be either game updates, competition announcements or match recaps.

## News

All the news will be displayed on this page. The posts can be filtered with the following tags:

- Campaign (teasers, seasonal additions)
- Update (patch notes)
- Event (collaborations, big community events)
- Nadeo 
- Esports

You can access the RSS feed for the news page here: https://blog.trackmania.com/rss
